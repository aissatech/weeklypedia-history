Hello there! Welcome once again to our weekly digest of Wikipedia activity. But first some exciting news. We at Hatnote helped launch a brand new official integration between Wikipedia and IFTTT. Whether you want more Wikipedia in your inbox, increased engagement on your home Wikipedia, or just plain love automation, there's something for you.
